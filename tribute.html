A.P.J Abdul Kalam Azad

Missile Man Of India

Here is a time line of Dr Kalam's Life

1914 - Born in Rameswaram, Madras Presidency

1954 - Graduated in Physics from Madras University

1955 - Moved to Madras to study Aerospace Engineering from Madras Institue of Technology

1960 - Joined the Aeronautical Development Estblishment of Defence Research And Development Organisation(DRDO) as a Scientist

1969 - Was transfered to Indian Space reasearch Organisation

1980 - Succesfully deployed Rohini using Satellte Launch Vehicle in near earth Orbit

1981 - Was Awarded Padam Bhushan

1993 - Launched the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV)

1994 - Began serving as chief Defence Advisor to Prime Minister and Secretory of DRDO

1997 - Was awarded Bharat Ratna (India's highest civilian award)

1998 - Developed low cost coronary stent along with Soma Raju

2002 - Was Appointed as the 11th President of India

2009 - Awarded Hoover Medal by ASME foundation USA

2013 - Awarded Von Brown Award by National Space Society

2015 - Died at age of 84 while giving at lecture at IIM Shillong

"Suitably named 'the People's President', Dr. Kalam's humility and dedication to public service served as an inspiration to millions of Indians and admirers around the world."

-U.S President Barack Obama
